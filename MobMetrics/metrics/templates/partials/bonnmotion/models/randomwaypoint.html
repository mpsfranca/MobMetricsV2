How to use RandomWaypoint
Dimension mode (-o) Optional

Defines the movement mode used to choose the next waypoint.

  • Enter Integer from 1 to 5
    Default: 4
  • 1 = movement only on x-axis
    2 = movement on x or y-axis
    3 = movement on x and y
    4 = movement on x or y or z-axis
    5 = movement on x and y and z, classical Random Waypoint in 3D
Examples:
-o 3
